Who we are

About The PTO

Bradshaw Mountain Performing Bears PTO is the volunteer-run booster organization behind the BMHS band, guard, and percussion programs. We run the fundraising, coordinate volunteers, and cover the real cost of a competitive program: instruments, uniforms, travel, competition fees, and equipment repair. As a registered 501(c)3, every dollar donated to the PTO is tax deductible and stays with the students.
